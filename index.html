    About Us

    It's time for Jaypee University of Information Technology's yearly literary extravaganza – a non-stop 48-hour marathon of literary delights that will captivate your attention around the clock. Brace yourself for an event that guarantees endless entertainment and absolutely no sleep. The festival kicks off with a grand opening ceremony, doubling as a musical evening, providing a stage for individuals to showcase their creativity through poetry, stories, and skits. Get ready for two days filled with excitement, creativity, and a complete lack of shut-eye.

    ABOUT LITERARY AND DEBATING CLUB

    The Literary & Debating Club serves as a vibrant hub for individuals passionate about literature, budding poets, and aspiring future leaders. Dedicated to fostering the comprehensive growth of students, this club is a nurturing ground for honing essential skills. It goes beyond the pages of books, encouraging members to cultivate self-confidence in public speaking and to refine their writing abilities. Through a dynamic range of activities, the club creates a conducive environment where literary enthusiasts can explore, express, and develop into well-rounded individuals. By embracing the twin pillars of eloquence and creativity, the Literary & Debating Club is a catalyst for empowering students on their journey of personal and intellectual evolution.
